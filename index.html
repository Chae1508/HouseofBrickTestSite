About House of Bricks

Your trusted partner in construction supplies

Leading Supplier in KwaZulu-Natal

House Of Bricks was started in 2022 due to the rising need of high quality brick products that meet industry standards!

House of Bricks is a major supplier in the building and construction supply industry. We supply home owners, small to large developers and hardware shops throughout KwaZulu-Natal.

We are an online store with a representative that travels throughout KwaZulu-Natal servicing our customers and ensuring customer satisfaction is met on every delivery.
